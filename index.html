    Who We Are

    Quran and Sunnah Academy (QNSA) is a non-profit organization registered in Canada. QNSA aims to provide Islamic knowledge to remind Muslims about their religious obligations to Allah, to themselves, to their families, neighbours and society, while working to strength individuals’ Islamic identity and feelings of belonging. QNSA also aims to educate the public about Islam through the authentic sources of the Quran & Sunnah to provide better understandings of Islam and Muslims.
